Data frames for R Map, converted from shape files.

* ND whole area (no subdivision)
* ND River5: ND whole rivers whose order is greater than 5.

* Separated ND River files
  * ND_str_ord_5: Stream order 5
  * ND_str_ord_6: Stream order 6
  * ND_str_ord_7: Stream order 7
  * ND_str_ord_8: Stream order 8
  * ND_str_ord_9: Stream order 9

* Study Sites: Sampling Sites and Barrage(Weir) location
* Dam Location: Dam
* Gauging Location: Gauging stations
* Weather Location: Weather stations
